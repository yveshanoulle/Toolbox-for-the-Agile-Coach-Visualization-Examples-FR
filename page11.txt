Avatars

Visualize who is working on which task with the use of avatars. If you work on a task, put your avatar on that task. The simplest version is names on small Post-its. More elaborate alternatives includes writing the initials on round magnets. But way more fun is to have photos of the team members. Even though I find it confusing, many teams like characters, such as South Park figures or cartoons.
